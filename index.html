Top three places to visit in Almaty

Kok Tobe is a mountain in Almaty, Kazakhstan's largest city. Kók Tóbe Park, a popular recreation area, is on top of the mountain.

The Medeu is a high-altitude sports complex near Almaty, located at an altitude of 1691 meters above sea level

The Ascension Cathedral also known as Zenkov Cathedral, is a Russian Orthodox cathedral located in Panfilov Park in Almaty.
